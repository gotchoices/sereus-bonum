# Bonum Requirements

## Primary Goals

The primary goal is to maintain a **ledger of transactions** from which any standard accounting report may be generated.

### Core Reports
- Income Statement (Profit & Loss)
- Balance Sheet
- Cash Flow Statement

### Core Capabilities
- Define multiple separate entities (businesses, households, etc.)
- Record transactions with double-entry accounting
- Tag entries orthogonally to accounts (for cost analysis)
- Reconcile accounts against external statements
- Support multiple currencies

## Secondary Goals

### Contact Management
- Vendor database
- Customer database
- Generalized partner/contact entities

### Receivables & Payables
- Accounts receivable tracking (linked to customers)
- Accounts payable tracking (linked to vendors)

## Advanced Features (Future)

These are potential capabilities for later phases:

- **Capital Asset Tracking:** Depreciation schedules, asset registers
- **Inventory Tracking:** Stock levels, FIFO/LIFO costing
- **Percent Completion Accruals:** For job costing and contract accounting
- **Budgeting:** Compare actual vs. planned figures
- **Multi-Entity Consolidation:** Roll up multiple business units into combined reports
